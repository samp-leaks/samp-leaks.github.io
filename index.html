About SAMP

SA-MP/San Andreas Multiplayer is a mod for playing Grand Theft Auto San Andreas in online multiplayer. You need a client to be able to play SA-MP. There are many gamemodes that can be played in SA-MP, such as Roleplay, Freeroam, Deathmatch, Race, Stunt, Zombie, and others.
